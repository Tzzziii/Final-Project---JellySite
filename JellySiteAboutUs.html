About Us

Our organization is dedicated to making a positive impact in our our oceans and preserving our stingy jelly friends. We are a team of amazingly ardent individuals who strive to create lasting change and interest in ocean life through our programs and services. Our mission is to make a meaningful difference in the the ocean for our Scyphozoa friends by promoting knowledge, care, and and proactiveness.
