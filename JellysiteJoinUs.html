Why should you join us?

When you join Jellysite, you're not just joining a website, you're joining a family. You're joining an organization committed to saving our many-tentacle'd friends from environmental issues in the ocean. More importantly, you're providing them a second chance at life. You can join us as a supporter by donating to our cause, or by volunteering to help clean our oceans and beaches. You can also join us by creatiing an account and signing up for our newsletter to get the latest news on when our team is on the move, when we have seminars near you, when new merchandise drops in our shop, or just cool Jellyfish funfacts!
